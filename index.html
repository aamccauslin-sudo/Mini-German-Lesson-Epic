German Greetings 👋

Guten Morgen!
Good Morning!
(GOO-ten MOR-gen)
Guten Tag!
Good Day! / Hello!
(GOO-ten TAHK)
Guten Abend!
Good Evening!
(GOO-ten AH-bent)

💡 Cultural Tip

Germans value politeness and formality in greetings. When meeting someone for the first time or in a professional setting, use "Sie" (formal you) instead of "du" (informal you).

Introducing Your Name 🎯

Ich heiße...
My name is... / I am called...
(ish HY-seh)
Ich heiße Sarah.
My name is Sarah.
Ich heiße Michael.
My name is Michael.

Fill in the blank:

How to Ask Questions ❓

Wie heißt du?
What's your name?
(vee heist doo)
Wie alt bist du?
How old are you?
(vee alt bist doo)
Was sind deine Hobbies?
What are your hobbies?
(vahs zint DY-neh HOH-bees)
Woher kommst du?
Where are you from?
(voh-HEHR kohmst doo)

💡 Language Tip

"Du" vs "Sie": Use "du" (informal you) with friends, family, and people your age. Use "Sie" (formal you) with adults, teachers, and in professional settings. When in doubt, start with "Sie"!
